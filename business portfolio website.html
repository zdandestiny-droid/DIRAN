Our Projects

Student Survival Survey

Conducted an economic survey among on-campus students to understand how they manage their finances, feeding, and academic life. The project involved data collection, statistical analysis, and presentation of findings that revealed how students balance learning with daily living challenges.

Numerical Solutions to Differential Equations

Worked on solving differential equations using numerical methods to explore their real-world applications in science and engineering. The project demonstrated our mathematical problem-solving and computational modeling abilities.

Probability Analysis

Completed various probability exercises and simulations to deepen our understanding of random events and distributions. This project enhanced our skills in statistical reasoning and practical application of probability theories.
